Hi, I’m Luke. I use Data Analytics and GIS to solve problems.

I love solving problems! That is what drew me to GIS and data analytics, and it is what continues to drive the way I approach my work. I strongly believe that good data and solid analysis can be the difference between a company that succeeds and one that falls behind. To me, GIS is about far more than making maps. When used effectively, it can move beyond a support function and become a strategic tool that directly shapes business decisions. That is why I see GIS and data analytics as the perfect pairing: both are ultimately about turning complex information into clear, data-driven action. I am currently pairing my B.S. in Geospatial Science and Technology with an M.S. in Data Analytics from Georgia Tech.
